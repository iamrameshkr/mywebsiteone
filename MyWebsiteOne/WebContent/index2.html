About

On the surface I think, I am not like most young and modern Indian Guys. I have never taken my life seriously, But I have dreams and goals for the future that I am determined to make happen, and I come from what is an increasingly normal background.

Even more

I live in Gole Market, Delhi which is the capital of India. Actually I come from another city - Ballia, a district of Uttar Pradesh where I was born. Since my father job is transferable, earlier we have to move to a new city after every 3 years. But now we have shifted in New Delhi, just my father keeps on moving alone. My father is a government employee and my mother is a housewife. I feel very lucky to have such supportive and lovely parents ,and always thank God for the same and pray to God for their heath and happiness. I have a brother . He is 21 and he is also in New Delhi and now studying MCA at the IP University.
